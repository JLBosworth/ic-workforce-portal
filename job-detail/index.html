Position Summary

The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) is seeking a highly qualified Supervisory Cybersecurity Analyst to lead threat intelligence operations within the National Cyber Threat Operations Center. You will manage a team of analysts responsible for identifying, tracking, and neutralizing advanced persistent threats targeting critical U.S. infrastructure sectors.

This is a leadership-critical position operating at the intersection of federal cyber defense, inter-agency coordination, and national security policy. The ideal candidate brings deep technical expertise in threat intelligence platforms, a proven track record managing analyst teams, and the ability to brief senior government officials on complex threat landscapes.

Key Duties & Responsibilities
01
Team LeadershipSupervise, mentor, and develop a team of 6–12 cyber threat intelligence analysts. Assign workloads, conduct performance evaluations, and foster a culture of continuous learning and excellence.
02
Threat Intelligence OperationsOversee the collection, analysis, and dissemination of actionable threat intelligence regarding APTs, ransomware groups, and state-sponsored cyber actors targeting U.S. critical infrastructure sectors.
03
Inter-Agency CoordinationServe as primary liaison with NSA, FBI Cyber Division, and sector-specific ISACs. Coordinate intelligence sharing under established frameworks including ISA, EO 14028, and M-21-31.
04
Briefings & ReportingPrepare and deliver regular threat briefings to senior CISA leadership, DHS Secretary staff, and Congressional liaison offices. Produce classified and unclassified intelligence products and advisories.
05
Platform & Tool ManagementManage and optimize threat intelligence platforms (TIPs), SIEM environments, and threat hunting infrastructure. Evaluate and recommend new technologies to enhance analytical capabilities.
Qualifications & Requirements
Required

Specialized Experience (GS-13: 1 year at GS-12)

At least one year of specialized experience at the GS-12 level (or equivalent) managing or leading a team conducting cyber threat intelligence analysis, incident response, or information security operations in a federal, military, or equivalent environment.

Required

Active TS/SCI Security Clearance

Applicants must possess a current Top Secret security clearance with Sensitive Compartmented Information (SCI) eligibility at the time of application. Clearance must remain active throughout employment.

Required

U.S. Citizenship

This position is open to U.S. citizens only. Dual citizenship may be a disqualifying factor depending on the countries involved. Contact HR for guidance before applying.

Preferred

Relevant Certifications

CISSP, GCIA, GCTI, CISM, or equivalent professional certifications are strongly preferred. Demonstrated proficiency with MITRE ATT&CK, STIX/TAXII, and enterprise TIP platforms (Recorded Future, Mandiant Advantage, etc.).

Preferred

Supervisory Experience

Prior federal supervisory experience or equivalent leadership of an intelligence analysis team. Completion of an OPM-approved supervisory training program preferred within 12 months of placement.
